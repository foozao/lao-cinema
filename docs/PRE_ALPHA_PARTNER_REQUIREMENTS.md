# Pre-Alpha Partner Requirements

This document tracks what we need from potential filmmaker and content partners to onboard their films to Lao Cinema. The goal is to gather everything necessary while keeping the process as frictionless as possible.

## Content Delivery

### Video Files
- [ ] **Master video files** (highest quality available)
  - Preferred formats: MP4, MOV, MKV
  - Minimum resolution: 1080p (4K preferred if available)
  - Delivery method: USB drive, cloud storage link (Google Drive, Dropbox), or direct upload
  - We handle transcoding to HLS streaming format

### Trailers
- [ ] **Official trailer(s)** (if available)
  - YouTube link OR video file
  - Multiple trailers welcome (teaser, full trailer, international versions)

### Images
- [ ] **Poster** (official movie poster)
  - High resolution (minimum 2000px on longest side)
  - Format: JPG or PNG
- [ ] **Backdrop/Banner** (wide landscape image for hero sections)
  - Aspect ratio: 16:9 or wider
  - High resolution (minimum 1920x1080)
- [ ] **Additional stills** (optional but recommended)
  - Production stills, behind-the-scenes photos
  - Help showcase the film visually

## Film Metadata

### Basic Information
- [ ] **Title** (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Tagline** (English and Lao, if available)
- [ ] **Overview/Synopsis** (English and Lao)
  - Short version (1-2 sentences)
  - Long version (full synopsis)
- [ ] **Release date** (original theatrical/festival release)
- [ ] **Runtime** (in minutes)
- [ ] **Genres** (e.g., Drama, Comedy, Action)
- [ ] **Language(s)** spoken in the film
- [ ] **Country of origin**
- [ ] **Production company name(s)**

### Cast & Crew

#### Director(s)
- [ ] **Name** (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Photo** (headshot, high resolution)
- [ ] **Bio** (English and Lao, optional)
- [ ] **Contact information** (for potential interviews/features)

#### Main Cast (top 5-10 actors)
For each actor:
- [ ] **Name** (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Character name** (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Photo** (headshot, high resolution)
- [ ] **Bio** (English and Lao, optional)
- [ ] **Contact information** (for potential interviews/features)
- [ ] **Order** (billing order/importance)

#### Key Crew
- [ ] **Writer(s)** - Name (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Producer(s)** - Name (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Cinematographer** - Name (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Editor** - Name (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Composer** - Name (English and Lao)
- [ ] **Other notable crew** (production designer, costume designer, etc.)

## Business & Legal

### Pricing & Revenue
- [ ] **Rental price preference** (USD)
  - What do you feel is fair to charge viewers?
  - Standard range: $2.99 - $6.99 for 48-hour rental
  - We can discuss revenue sharing models
- [ ] **Rental duration preference** (24h, 48h, 72h, 7 days?)
- [ ] **Geographic restrictions** (if any)
  - Worldwide? Specific regions only?
- [ ] **Exclusive vs. non-exclusive**
  - Can film be on other platforms simultaneously?

### Rights & Permissions
- [ ] **Distribution rights confirmation**
  - Do you own/control digital distribution rights?
  - Any existing platform exclusivity agreements?
- [ ] **Music licensing** (if applicable)
  - Are all music rights cleared for streaming?
- [ ] **Subtitle/caption files** (if available)
  - SRT or VTT format
  - English, Lao, or other languages

## Analytics & Reporting

### What metrics matter to you?
- [ ] **View counts** (total plays, unique viewers)
- [ ] **Watch completion rate** (% who finish the film)
- [ ] **Average watch time**
- [ ] **Geographic distribution** (where viewers are located)
- [ ] **Revenue reports** (rental income over time)
- [ ] **Audience demographics** (age, gender - if we collect this)
- [ ] **Peak viewing times** (when people watch)
- [ ] **Device breakdown** (mobile vs. desktop vs. TV)
- [ ] **Traffic sources** (how viewers found the film)
- [ ] **Other specific metrics?**

### Reporting Frequency
- [ ] How often would you like reports?
  - Weekly, monthly, quarterly?
  - Real-time dashboard access?

## Platform Feedback

### Website & User Experience
- [ ] **First impressions** of the platform
- [ ] **Navigation** - Is it easy to find films?
- [ ] **Design** - Does it feel professional and appealing?
- [ ] **Mobile experience** - How does it work on phones/tablets?
- [ ] **Video player** - Quality, controls, performance?
- [ ] **Payment flow** - Is the rental process clear and easy?
- [ ] **Language switching** - Does bilingual support work well?

### Feature Requests
- [ ] **Missing features** - What would make this better for you?
- [ ] **Marketing tools** - What would help you promote your film?
  - Shareable links, embed codes, press kit downloads?
- [ ] **Community features** - Comments, ratings, reviews?
- [ ] **Festival/event support** - Virtual screenings, Q&A integration?

  - High-res images, synopsis, cast/crew info
- [ ] **Reviews/press coverage** (links or PDFs)
  - Festival awards, critic reviews, press mentions
- [ ] **Social media assets**
  - Approved clips, GIFs, quote cards
- [ ] **Behind-the-scenes content**
  - Making-of videos, interviews, featurettes

- [ ] **Official film social media** (if exists)
  - Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube
- [ ] **Your social media** (filmmaker accounts)
- [ ] **Website** (if you have a film website)
- [ ] **Preferred contact method**
  - Email, phone, messaging app?

### Launch Strategy
- [ ] **Preferred launch date** (if you have one in mind)
- [ ] **Announcement timing** - When can we start promoting?
- [ ] **Exclusive premiere period?** (featured placement for X days)
- [ ] **Cross-promotion opportunities**
  - Can we collaborate on social media posts?
  - Press release coordination?

## Technical Considerations

### Subtitles & Accessibility
- [ ] **Subtitle files** (if you have them)
  - Languages available
  - Format: SRT, VTT, or embedded
- [ ] **Closed captions** (for accessibility)
- [ ] **Audio descriptions** (for visually impaired, if available)
- [ ] **Dubbed versions** (if multiple audio tracks exist)

### Quality Control
- [ ] **Preferred aspect ratio** (if specific)
  - 16:9, 2.35:1, 2.39:1, etc.
- [ ] **Color grading notes** (any specific requirements)
- [ ] **Audio mix** (stereo, 5.1, Atmos?)
- [ ] **Credits** - Full credits list (if not in video)

## Timeline & Next Steps

### Onboarding Process
1. **Initial contact** - Introductions and interest confirmation
2. **Content review** - We review film and materials
3. **Agreement** - Discuss terms, pricing, revenue share
4. **Content delivery** - Receive video files and assets
5. **Platform setup** - We upload, transcode, and configure
6. **Review & approval** - You review how it looks on platform
7. **Launch coordination** - Set date and promotional plan
8. **Go live** - Film becomes available to viewers
9. **Ongoing** - Analytics, support, updates

### Questions for You
- [ ] **Timeline** - When would you like to launch?
- [ ] **Urgency** - Is this time-sensitive (festival tie-in, anniversary, etc.)?
- [ ] **Support needs** - Do you need help with any of the above?
- [ ] **Concerns** - Any worries or questions about the platform?
